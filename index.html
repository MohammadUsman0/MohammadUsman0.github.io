Usman Mohammad

PhD Researcher in Deep Learning & Computer Vision

I am a predoctoral researcher at the Mathematics and Computer Sciences Department of the University of Barcelona, specializing in Deep Learning and Computer Vision in Barcelona, Spain. My research focuses on leveraging advanced AI techniques to solve complex problems at the intersection of these fields.

With a strong background as a Machine Learning Engineer, I have a demonstrated history of working in AI within the medical domain. My expertise includes Python, R, and related technologies. I hold an Honours Master's degree in Computer Science from COMSATS University Islamabad (2021) and a BSCS from Islamia University Bahawalpur (2018).
