Usman Mohammad

I am a predoctoral researcher at the Mathematics and Computer Sciences Department of the University of Barcelona, specializing in **Deep Learning, Genomics, and Computer Vision** in Barcelona, Spain. My research focuses on leveraging advanced AI techniques to solve complex problems at the intersection of these fields.

With a strong background as a Machine Learning Engineer, I have a demonstrated history of working in AI within the medical domain. My expertise includes Python, R, and related technologies. I hold an Honours Master's degree in Computer Science from COMSATS University Islamabad (2021) and a BSCS from Islamia University Bahawalpur (2018).

Research Interests

My primary research interests lie at the intersection of **Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Image Processing**. I am particularly focused on developing robust and interpretable AI models for complex biological and medical data.
